Why Managing Multiple Stores Gets Harder Every Quarter

One store runs fine on a spreadsheet. Two or more, and the gaps grow fast: each location closes at a different time, tickets activated at one store get sold at another with no paper trail, and discrepancies only surface at month-end when the damage is already done. Without proper multi store inventory management software in place, these quiet losses drain 3-8% off your monthly lottery revenue. Generic inventory tools were never built to catch lottery-specific leaks across multiple locations - that is where most chains bleed money.
