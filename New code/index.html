About LAI 5 Lottery Management Software

LAI 5 is lottery management software built specifically for retail lottery operators - gas stations, liquor stores, smoke shops, newsstands, grocery stores, and any business that sells instant lottery tickets. It replaces manual tracking with an AI lottery software system that handles inventory, reconciliation, theft alerts, and shift reports automatically. LAI 5 runs on Windows, Android, and iOS simultaneously and supports operations across all 50 states.

LAI 5 lottery tracking software works in three steps at the end of every shift:

1. Scan - Staff scan ending ticket numbers using the LAI 5 wireless scanner or smartphone camera. The system logs each scan and updates your lottery inventory management database instantly.
2. Analyze - The AI engine reviews scan data, flags any discrepancies for lottery theft prevention, and generates real-time lottery sales tracking data across all your games.
3. Report - Complete automated shift reports - total sales, money drops, inventory status, and alerts - are emailed to you automatically within seconds. No manual input required.

LAI 5 runs on Windows desktops and laptops, Android smartphones and tablets, and iPhones and iPads. As a cloud-based retail lottery software platform, the full dashboard is accessible from any modern web browser - no installation required for mobile access. This cross-platform flexibility means you can manage your multi-store lottery management operations from any device, anywhere in the world, 24 hours a day.
